Shamu Shish Kebab

Sorry about this

Delicious kebabs made out of grilled, beloved whale

We all love Shamu. Shamu, the whale from Seaworld that does tricks? Turns out Seaworld is more insidious than previously thought. Recently they opened up a new onsite restaurant, "Shamu's Grill Shack". Patrons of the restaurant raved about the uniquely seasoned and flavored meat that was sold there. An undercover investigative journalist broke news that their cheap and savory shish kebabs were actually made out of their beloved whale performer, Shamu! Amidst public outcry, a recipe for these kebabs was discreetly released for those that were curious. Here it is below.

Ingredients

  • Shamu

Steps

  1. Cut Shamu up into evenly sized filets
  2. Season with salt and pepper, melt 1tbsp of butter in pan and sear on medium high for 4 min per side
  3. After sear, cover in thinly sliced lemon slices, and bake in oven at 350F for 20 min
  4. Garnish with Parsley, and serve with a side of asparagus
