Sloppy Steaks

From: I think you should Leave by Tim Robinson

Some rowdy boys slopping up some steaks

Have you ever wondered how Tim Robinson and his boys created such imacculate sloppy steaks? We'll teach you how in this wonderful recipe.

I remember when I was a young little boy and my grandmother would use to make sloppy steaks every morning. I would wake up early at 7am to the sound of ice cold water being poured over some perfectly coooked and sizzling sloppy steaks. Memories like that never fade, and so I am here to share the recipe with you today.

Ingredients

  • Steaks
  • Water (Slop)

Steps

  1. Throw the steaks on a pan on medium high heat. Cook for 6 minutes on boths sides
  2. Put the sizzling hot steaks on a ceramic plate, and pour water over it.
