Pretty Patties

Created by Spongebob Squarepants

Spongebob holding some beautiful pretty patties

Spongebob, like Steve Jobs, was a genius who was ahead of his time. Initially ridiculed for his idea to create krabby patties of different colors, he found immense success after stringent market analysis, and altering his product to meet consumer demands.

Spongebob's pretty patties may seem difficult to create. And they are. The best we can do is a pale imitation of them. Here's my best attempt at a recipe:

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Krabby Patty (Made out of REDACTED)
  • Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Assemble Krabby Patty in this order: Bottoom bun, mayo, patty, lettuce, cheese, onion, tomatoes, ketchup, mustard, pickles, and top bun.
  2. Dip in Food Coloring of Choice
